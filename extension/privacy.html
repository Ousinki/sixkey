⠿Privacy Policy

SixKey Braille – 盲文悬浮注音

Overview

SixKey Braille ("the Extension") is a browser extension that displays Chinese braille (现行盲文) annotations when you hover over Chinese text on web pages. Your privacy is important to us.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

The Extension operates entirely locally within your browser. Specifically:

  • No user data is sent to any external server.
  • No browsing history, page content, or personal information is collected.
  • No analytics, tracking, or telemetry of any kind is used.
  • No cookies are read or set by the Extension.

Permissions Used

  • storage — Used solely to save your preferences (e.g., tone display on/off, theme choice). These settings are stored locally in Chrome's chrome.storage.sync and are never sent to any third party.

Third-Party Libraries

The Extension includes pinyin-pro (an open-source Pinyin conversion library) which runs entirely in the browser with no network requests.

Content Scripts

The Extension injects content scripts into web pages to detect Chinese text under your mouse cursor and display braille annotations. All processing happens locally — no page content is ever transmitted externally.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page and the "Last updated" date below.
